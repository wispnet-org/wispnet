Home

The WISPNET Test Development Site (TDS) serves as a workspace for testing and improving code before release. It evolves continuously as part of our mission to experiment and learn.

About

WISPNET is an experimental local network created by William S. Popovici, designed for repairing, utilizing, and learning about technology through hands-on projects.

Services

We are developing Linux Mint-Basil, a lightweight OS optimized for older systems like Dell OptiPlex and Raspberry Pi. Future web design and open-source services are in progress.

Projects

Explore ongoing initiatives such as IOserver, a collection of JavaScript-based single-player games for learning and entertainment.
